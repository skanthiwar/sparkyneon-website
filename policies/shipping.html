Shipping Policy

Your order journey from checkout to doorstep — safe, fast, and trackable.

Order Processing

At Sparky Neon, every sign is made with precision and care. Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Orders placed after 5 PM IST are processed the following business day.

Shipping Coverage

We proudly ship across all regions of India. For international orders or shipping to remote areas, please contact our team at hello@sparkyneon.in for support and availability.

Estimated Delivery Time

  • Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days after dispatch
  • Express Shipping: 2–4 business days (additional charges may apply)

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS for real-time updates. You can also track the status from your account dashboard on our website.

Shipping Delays

We aim to deliver all orders on time. However, delays caused by weather, high demand, or courier issues may occur. In such cases, our team will notify you proactively.

Need Help?

If you have questions or concerns about shipping, feel free to reach out at hello@sparkyneon.in or call us at +91 98765 43210. We're here to help!
