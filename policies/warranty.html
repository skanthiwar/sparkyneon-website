Warranty Policy

We stand behind the quality of our LED neon signs with a commitment to reliability and service.

Warranty Coverage

All Sparky Neon signs come with a 12-month limited warranty from the date of delivery. This covers manufacturing defects and electrical faults under normal usage conditions.

What’s Not Covered

Warranty does not apply in the following cases:

  • Accidental damage, misuse, or neglect
  • Damage from incorrect installation
  • Normal wear and tear, or unauthorized alterations

Claim Process

If you experience an issue, email hello@sparkyneon.in with your order number, product photos/videos, and a description of the problem. Our team will assist you with a replacement or repair.

Repair & Replacement

Depending on the situation, we may offer:

  • Repair instructions or kits
  • A full or partial replacement
  • Store credit or refund (in rare cases)
