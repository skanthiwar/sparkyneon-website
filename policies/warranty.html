Warranty

Your purchase is backed by assurance — learn more about our warranty coverage and terms.

Warranty Coverage

All Sparky Neon products are covered under a 12-month limited warranty from the date of delivery. This warranty includes defects in materials or workmanship under normal usage conditions.

What’s Not Covered

  • Damage from misuse, mishandling, or improper installation
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Cosmetic wear and tear

Warranty Process

To request a warranty claim, email hello@sparkyneon.in with your order number and a description (with photos/videos) of the issue. Our support team will assess and guide you through the resolution.

Replacement & Repair

If your product qualifies under warranty, we will either repair or replace it at no additional cost. In some cases, a partial or full refund may be provided depending on the situation.

Additional Notes

Warranty is valid only for the original purchaser. Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

Need help with a warranty claim? Contact us at hello@sparkyneon.in or call +91 98765 43210. We're happy to assist you!
